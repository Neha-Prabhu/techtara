About

From the earliest days of computer science, women have made crucial contributions to its progress. As our world today moves towards a brighter and more equal future, it is time we sit up and take notice of these contributions. Organizations around the globe are pushing for more recognition for women in technology, and young girls are being encouraged to take up technology as a career choice. In keeping with this spirit, the Department of Computer Science & Engineering, Chaitanya Bharathi Institute of Technology hosted its first event focused on the role of women in technology in 2019.

We bring forward this grandeur by organizing Techtara 2020 with the theme “Educate, Empower, Evolve”. TechTara will feature a diverse and eminent panel of speakers, who will deliver talks and participate in panel discussions. Other events will also be conducted to help students identify and display their skills.

OBJECTIVES

  • To promote a global neutral space that works towards the involvement of women in technology.
  • To ensure education of women and mentor them to take up roles in technology.
  • To create a supoortive environment for women aspiring to work in technology.
  • To motivate women to take up roles of leadership in society.
  • To ensure and improve safety of women as a community.

What can I expect?

  • Inspiring talks by women in the fields of technology and society.
  • Panel discussions involving the speakers.
  • Mentoring of the students to motivate them to take up roles in technology
  • Fun activities involving speakers and students.
  • Coding competitions with exciting prizes for the winners.

When is the Event?

TechTara is based at Chaitanya Bharathi Institute of Technology, Hyderabad on 19th and 20th of March.

What is the cost of the Symposium?

This "Two Day Conference" costs include all the material, stationery and meals for the entirety of the program.

Ticket Price: ₹ 200/- for two days.
